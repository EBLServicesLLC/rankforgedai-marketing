Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Introduction

RankForged AI ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by EBL Services LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our local SEO platform at app.rankforgedai.com.

By using RankForged AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (encrypted).

Business Information: Information you provide about your business including name, address, phone number, website, business category, description, and keywords.

API Keys: Third-party API keys you enter (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, etc.) are stored encrypted in our database and used solely to perform SEO services on your behalf.

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our platform including pages visited, features used, and actions taken.

Payment Information: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the RankForged AI platform
  • To authenticate your account and maintain security
  • To perform SEO audits, rank tracking, and content generation on your behalf
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, billing receipts)
  • To improve and optimize our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security (RLS) enabled. This means your data is logically isolated and only accessible by your authenticated account.

API keys and sensitive credentials are stored encrypted. We use HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

RankForged AI integrates with the following third-party services. Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Vercel — Application hosting
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery
  • Google APIs — Search Console, Business Profile integration
  • Anthropic / OpenAI / Gemini — AI content generation (when API keys provided)
  • LinkedIn — Social media publishing (when connected)

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
